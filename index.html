Our Story

Modern Day
Popular.

BumbleBear was born from a simple obsession: finding the coolest, most sought-after items before everyone else does. Rooted right here in East Northport, Long Island, we're the neighborhood shop that moves at the speed of culture.

We track what's trending — on TikTok, YouTube, and beyond — and make sure our shelves reflect what kids, collectors, and curious adults actually want. No filler. Only the good stuff, curated with genuine love for this community.

Whether you're a seasoned collector hunting for a rare blind box or a parent trying to nail that birthday gift, walking into BumbleBear should feel like a discovery every single time.
